### 1. Variables
* *Assignment* uses the `=` operator: `x = 5`
* Variable names are **case-sensitive**, must start with a letter/underscore, and cannot be keywords.

### 2. Expressions & Operators
| Operator | Purpose | Example | Result |
|----------|---------|---------|--------|
| `+ - * /` | arithmetic | `3 * 4` | 12 |
| `**` | exponent | `2 ** 3` | 8 |
| `//` | floor division | `7 // 3` | 2 |
| `%` | remainder | `7 % 3` | 1 |
